Installation Guide

1. Local AI

  • 1 Install Ollama on your machine.
  • 2 Run ollama run qwen2.5-vl in your terminal.
  • 3 The app will connect to your local AI automatically.

2. macOS App

  • 1 Download the `.zip` archive for Apple Silicon.
  • 2 Move the app to your Applications folder.
  • 3 Right-click and select Open to launch.

3. Extension

  • 1 Unzip the extension and go to chrome://extensions.
  • 2 Enable Developer Mode.
  • 3 Click Load unpacked and select the folder.
